DATABASE SCHEMA DOCUMENT

1) LEAD

Primary Key: - id

Fields: - firstName - lastName - email - phone - alternateNo -
dateOfBirth - gender - nationality - maritalStatus - address -
highestQualification - interestedCourse - testName - testScore -
interestedCountry - intake - applyLevel - source - status (default: New
Enquiry) - remark - createdAt - updatedAt

2) FOLLOW UP

Primary Key: - id

Foreign Key: - leadId (references Lead.id)

Fields: - followUpDate - followUpTime - followUpRemarks - createdAt

Relationship: - One Lead can have many Follow Ups

3) APPOINTMENT

Primary Key: - id

Foreign Key: - leadId (references Lead.id)

Fields: - appointmentDate - appointmentTime - appointmentRemarks -
createdAt

Relationship: - One Lead can have many Appointments
